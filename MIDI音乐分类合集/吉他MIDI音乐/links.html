Links to other classical guitar sites

Societies

  • Guitar Foundation of America: a lot of information about guitar performers, guitar makers, dates of concerts, expositions, etc.

  • Guitar Houston: sponsors concerts and masterclasses of well-known guitarists, upcoming events, links to other guitar sites.

  • Guitarra Magazine: a magazine containing a section on guitar museum, a catalog of guitars, strings, luthier supplies and other accessories; a picture gallery on guitarists performers and a section on coming festivals and competitions.

  • Ventura County Classical Guitar Society: this site is intended to be a source of information (local concerts, recitals, vendors) about the classical guitar in Ventura County.

Guitar music and supplies

  • Les Productions d'OZ: publishers, engravers of classical guitar music. Many related links of the world of classical guitar.
  • Stafford Classical Guitar Centre: sellers of high quality instruments in the classical guitar world. They promote classical guitar recital competition, concerts and classical guitar courses. They promote also young classical guitarists and written music by lesser-known composers.

  • Virtual Sheet Music - Classical Sheet Music Downloads: Selected scores for piano, violin, trumpet and other instruments ready to download and print out! You are welcome!

Guitar homepages

  • Amber Rose Guitar Music: Amber Rose Guitar Music was founded in 1990 out of a true desire to nourish and extend the guitar repertoire and to offer the world of ensemble playing much needed fresh material that is enjoyable, entertaining and educational. They offer a catalogue of musical arrangements and teaching material. Webmaster is Martin Nockalls.
  • Andrés Tapia, présentation de l'artiste: The site of Andres Tapia, guitarist from Chile living in Switzerland, performer and teacher. There is a page on his discography, the agenda of his concerts.

  • The Barrios Homepage: A biography and a collection of tabs, pictures, midis on the great composer Agustin Barrios.

  • La bibliothèque de la guitare classique: Site of Jean-François Delcamp, professor of classical guitar, performer. His site presents scores of the  repertoire of classical guitar. Also, a short biography of the composers of the instrument and links to the sites of the performers for guitar (in French).
  • Carolina Culture Connection: An Index of Classical Music Web Site Links. The sites on this page contain information on many historical periods, or they pertain to a single instrument's history and/or literature.
  • ChordWizard Music Theory: A six-part tutorial is a concise summary of the most important concepts in music. The tutorial assumes no prior knowledge of music, starting from the very fundamentals of music, but going right through to the use of advanced chords and scales.

  • Classe de guitare classique - Patrick Navarro:  In Bordeaux, France. Contains notes reading, rythm, solfa, technic, harmony, history of music with a museum on the history of guitar (in French).

  • Classical Compositions:  A site containing  many MIDI files listed by composer, plus links of sites containing MIDI.

  • Classical Guitar - Henk Pieters:  Information, a lot of music in MIDI format (different styles) and over 250 chords with sound. The construction, maintenance and repair of the classical guitar."
  • Classical Guitar - Eduardo Barrientos:  This site contains biographies of guitarists like Fernando Sor, Agustín Barrios, Leo Brouwer, Tarrega and others. You can also download MIDI Files for guitar of many  works of Sor, Villa-Lobos, and original works for Lute and Vihuela of the most celebrated composers of the 16th and 17th centuries.
  • Classical Guitar Aficionados' Page: advice for making a wise guitar purchase. Readers choose who and what are the best of the best. Introducing the first installment of Classical Guitar sheet music. A list of classical guitar societies around the world and playing guide.

  • Classical Guitar Home Page - Carlos Cordero: in this site you will find Classical Guitar Tablatures, Composers´ Bios, Multimedia Files, Pictures, Tablature Explanation, Comments, Links and others.

  • Classical Guitar Midi - Tom Ruppert: a very good page containing well sequenced MIDI from many classical guitar composers. 

  • The Classical Guitar Page of Nikita Boldyrev: This page contains a wide variety of classical guitar scores, tabs and music files. There are special pages devoted to Granados, Tarrega and Barrios-Mangore.

  • Claude Engel's Web Page: presents the biography, list of compositions and discography of the classical guitar composer throughout the 70's, 80's and 90's. Claude Engel has also published eight books containing 26 classical guitar compositions.

  • Concerthall by Arman: Classical composers database. Collections of MIDI and portraits of composers.
  • Gren Music: Gren Music Concert Group is an association of independent musicians who share common interests in Classical, Russian, Israeli and Klezmer music. They organize events worldwide.

  • La Guitarra Española: A web site on the history of spanish guitar with sections on the medieval music, the vihuela, the baroque guitar and the flamenco (in Spanish and English).
  • The J. S. Bach Home Page: A complete site on Bach. Contains biography, list of works, portraits of Bach. List of his works and recordings, including MIDI, on this great composer. Links on web sites on Bach.
  • Kenneth Sparr's Home Page: The guitar and music for the guitar in Sweden, the lute and its music in Sweden, tablature, other articles by Kenneth Sparr. Links to other sites on guitar and lute.

  • Michel Dalle Ave Fingerstyle Guitarist & Composer: The site of the guitar in Gironde (France). Free resources for guitarists: Midi, Mp3, Pdf, Tabledit and Guitar-Pro files,Links, Guitarists Collection, Tips (bilingual). 
  • Michelson Classic Guitar Studio: A site on teaching classic guitar to children with a method developed for them. The author has published articles and books on the subject. 

  • Milaresolsimi- le portail de la guitare acoustique-: Free music for guitarist : scores and Tablatures to formats Tabledit, Guitar-Pro, Gif, Pdf, Jpg etc..  
  • Perso Flamenco: Partitions Gratuites pour guitare flamenco et classique, 
    partituras, mp3 . Free    : Free Flamenco and classical guitar scores (audios (mp3)): Paco de Lucia, Albeniz, Sabicas, Federico Garcia Lorca, Pepe Habichuela, Juan Serrano, Granados, Manuel de Falla, Tomatito...

  • The Registry of Guitar Tutors: In association with the London College of Music, our mission is to improve vastly the status and standards for guitar in mainstream musical education.

  • Roland Ferrandi - passionné de luth : this is the site of an ancient music and instruments lover. Ancient instruments presented are Baroque guitar, Renaissance lute, theorbo and Corsican cittern. A technical approach on those instruments is presented. Illustrations and downloading of pieces in MP3 format is provided.

  • WS64 Guitar Chords: Graphical display of chord shapes, including 6ths, 9ths and other jazz chords. All chords available as MIDI-files.Plus many useful links for guitarists.
  • Your About.com Guide to MP3/MIDI music: a very interesting guide containing informations where to find midi files on different styles of music.

Miscellanneous

  • Guitar Pro: Guitar Pro is a multitrack tablature editor for guitar, banjo and bass. Besides writing scores, Guitar Pro is a complete tool for young and accomplished guitarists alike to improve their skills, compose, or simply accompany themselves.

  • TablEdit: TablEdit  is a program for creating, editing, printing and listening to tablature and sheet music (standard notation) for guitar and other fretted, stringed instruments.
